# 🎬 Prime Video Content Analysis Dashboard – Power BI Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive **Prime Video Content Analysis Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI**. The dashboard provides an in-depth exploration of Prime Video’s global content library, offering valuable insights into titles distribution, ratings classification, genre trends, country-wise availability, and release year growth patterns.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate strong **data visualization, data modeling, DAX calculations, and business intelligence skills** by transforming raw entertainment data into actionable insights.

## 📊 Key Dashboard Highlights

### 🔹 Content Overview Metrics

* **Total Titles:** 9,655
* **Total Ratings Categories:** 25
* **Total Genres:** 519
* **Total Directors:** 5,771
* **Content Timeline:** 1920 – 2021

These KPI cards provide a quick snapshot of the Prime Video catalog scale and diversity.

---

### 🌍 Country-wise Content Distribution

An interactive world map visual displays the distribution of total shows across different countries, enabling geographical analysis of content production and availability.

---

### 📈 Movies vs TV Shows Trend (Release Year Analysis)

A time-series analysis visual highlights:

* Growth of Movies vs TV Shows over decades
* Rapid expansion in content production after 2000
* Significant surge in recent years

This helps understand platform expansion and content investment strategy.

---

### 🎭 Genre Distribution Analysis

Top genres by total shows include:

* Drama
* Comedy
* Drama & Suspense
* Comedy & Drama
* Animation & Kids
* Documentary

This visualization helps identify audience-focused content categories.

---

### 🎥 Content Type Breakdown

A donut chart comparison between:

* **Movies (80.82%)**
* **TV Shows (19.18%)**

This clearly demonstrates Prime Video’s dominant content type.

---

### 🔞 Ratings Classification Analysis

The dashboard analyzes content distribution by rating categories such as:

* 13+
* 16+
* 18+
* PG-13
* Kids categories

This provides insight into target audience segmentation.

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

* **Microsoft Power BI**
* Power Query (Data Transformation)
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Modeling (Star Schema Approach)

---

## 📂 Business Insights Derived

* Prime Video’s content library has significantly expanded post-2000.
* Movies dominate the platform compared to TV Shows.
* Drama and Comedy are the most produced genres.
* Content is globally distributed but concentrated in key production countries.
* A large portion of content falls under 13+ and 16+ rating categories.
